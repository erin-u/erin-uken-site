About Erin

Authentic strategy, human connection, and meaningful momentum.

Erin Uken is a strategist, communicator, and guide who helps people and businesses move forward with clarity. Her work blends leadership, marketing, AI adoption, and real estate support into one clear story.

Mission

To help people and businesses create clarity, confidence, and momentum through authentic strategy and meaningful connection.

Vision

To build a future where trusted guidance, thoughtful technology, and human-centered storytelling help more people thrive.

Values

Authenticity, service, excellence, innovation, joy, and stewardship.

Embark • Embrace • Enjoy

These words frame the way Erin leads and serves: start with courage, receive what is unfolding, and create space for joy in the process. The first four were inspired by Eric Geiger's leadership work.

The 10 C's

A leadership framework of character and capability

Character

Lead with integrity, humility, and consistent values.

Competence

Bring skill, preparation, and thoughtful execution to every opportunity.

Chemistry

Create connection that feels natural, warm, and lasting.

Conviction

Stand firmly for what matters and act with purpose.

Curiosity

Stay open, learn fast, and welcome new possibilities.

Communication

Speak clearly, listen deeply, and make complex ideas simple.

Confidence

Move forward with calm assurance and grounded courage.

Compassion

Serve people with empathy, care, and respect.

Continuous Improvement

Keep evolving, refining, and raising the standard.

Community Stewardship

Use influence to strengthen people, place, and purpose.
